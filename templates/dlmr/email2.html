Flagler Schools
Media Parent Choice
School Year: 2023-2024

The district understands parents and guardians play a major role in guiding their child’s reading and media center use. It is important that each family has the right to determine which library resources are acceptable for its children and that we afford that right to each family. The district strives to provide appropriate educational, intellectual and physical access to resources that provide for a wide range of abilities and differing points of view. This process in no way negates our responsibility to ensure that the materials that are in our media centers align to state statutes but provides families with additional choice around all available media materials.

Choices:

Level 1: Open Access: There are some levels of books that require parent permission before a student can check them out.

Level 2: Limited Access: Families may submit a list of up to 5 books that students are NOT allowed to check-out. Outside of the 5 books that students are NOT allowed to check-out, students have the same access to Level 1.

Level 3: Pre-Approval: A parent/guardian will pre-approve all books before a student checks them out.
